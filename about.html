The Story

Charleston Treasures started as a simple idea—document the places I actually go, the meals I actually order, and the recipes I actually make at home. Being based in Charleston, South Carolina, I’ve always had access to incredible food, but what stood out most wasn’t just the big-name restaurants. It was the smaller moments—the perfect brunch, the go-to dinner spot, the recipe you keep coming back to—that made the city feel special.

This site is built around that idea. No overcomplication, no fluff—just real recommendations, simple recipes, and a growing list of places that make Charleston worth exploring again and again.

The Author

I’m a Charleston local with a simple routine—eat well, stay active, and keep things balanced. Most of my free time revolves around trying new restaurants, recreating meals at home, and keeping up with fitness in between.

Charleston Treasures reflects that lifestyle. It’s not about chasing trends—it’s about finding what’s consistently good, what’s worth repeating, and what makes everyday life in Charleston better.

If you’re looking for real restaurant picks, easy recipes, or just a reason to try something new around Charleston, this site is built for that.
